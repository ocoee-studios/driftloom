# DriftLoom Direction Change

## Decision

DriftLoom will no longer be merged with AuraLunis.

AuraLunis remains the dedicated dream, lucid dreaming, moon cycle, symbol interpretation, and subconscious exploration app.

DriftLoom will become a separate product focused on private personal capture, reflection, notes, ideas, memories, and pattern discovery.

---

## DriftLoom Vision

A beautiful, privacy-first personal notes app that helps users capture life's fragments and discover meaningful patterns over time.

Tagline:

"Capture the fragments. Find the pattern."

## Portfolio Strategy

AuraLunis = Dreams, symbols, lucid exploration.
