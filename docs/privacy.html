Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Your dreams are private. DriftLoom is designed to keep your data on your device. We collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored on your device only: Dream journal entries, mood data, check-in responses, sleep conditions, settings, themes, ink colors, fonts, passcode, and all personal preferences are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use an AI feature or export your data.

Data processed temporarily: When you use AI-powered features (dream interpretation, pattern analysis, bedtime stories, affirmations), your dream text is sent to our secure API for processing. This data is used solely to generate your result and is not stored on our servers after the response is delivered.

Data we do NOT collect: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any personal identifiers. We do not use tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or analytics frameworks that profile users.

2. Biometric Data

DriftLoom supports Face ID and Touch ID for app lock authentication. Biometric data is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. We never access, store, or transmit biometric data. Authentication results are a simple yes/no — we never see your face or fingerprint.

3. How We Use Information

Dream text sent to AI features is processed solely to provide you with interpretations, analysis, and personalized content. We do not use your dream content to train AI models, build user profiles, serve advertisements, or share with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All journal data is stored locally on your device. If you enable app lock, your data is protected by your chosen passcode and/or biometric authentication. We recommend keeping your device updated and using a strong device passcode for maximum security.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details. Apple's privacy policy governs payment processing.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Processing: Dream text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI features. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not retain user inputs for model training. No personally identifiable information is included in API requests.

Apple: The App Store handles downloads, subscriptions, and payments according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

DriftLoom is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Since your data is stored on your device, you have full control. You can delete individual dreams, reset your entire journal, or delete the app to remove all data. AI processing data is not retained on our servers after your response is delivered.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access all your data (it's on your device), delete your data at any time, export your dream journal, and opt out of AI features while continuing to use all free features. No account is required to use DriftLoom.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at privacy@ocoeestudios.com.
