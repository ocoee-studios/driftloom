## Description
Driftloom turns creative drift into direction.

Your best ideas don't arrive neatly organized — they come as half-formed thoughts, stray voice memos, screenshots, and shower epiphanies. Driftloom gives them somewhere to go and helps you find the project hiding inside.

CAPTURE ANYTHING
Drop in text notes, voice recordings, camera shots, links, and location-tagged ideas. Each one becomes a fragment in your personal creative loom.

FIND THE PATTERN
Driftloom automatically detects signals across your fragments. That app idea you keep circling? The aesthetic you screenshot every week? The problem you describe three different ways? Driftloom sees the connection.

INCUBATE IDEAS
Choose a creative mentor (designer, writer, founder, filmmaker, or game maker) and a direction (app, story, or brand). Driftloom synthesizes your fragments into a concept with a clear first move.

BUILD MODE
When you're ready, Build Mode generates a first-version spec: one screen, one promise, three steps, and a guardrail to keep you focused.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
Star favorites, add follow-up notes, set revisit reminders, search and filter — your fragments stay alive and evolving, not buried in folders.

SHARE YOUR THINKING
Email project briefs, build plans, and daily digests to yourself. Share individual fragments or full project summaries.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your ideas are yours.

FREE TO START
• 50 fragments and 3 projects on the free tier
• Daily spark prompts and basic pattern detection
• Upgrade to Plus for unlimited capture, mentors, build plans, and exports
