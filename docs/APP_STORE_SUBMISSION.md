## Description (4000 chars max)
Driftloom turns creative drift into direction.

Your best ideas don't arrive neatly organized — they come as half-formed thoughts, stray notes, screenshots, and shower epiphanies. Driftloom gives them somewhere to go and helps you find the project hiding inside.

CAPTURE ANYTHING
Drop in text notes, links, and stray ideas. Each one becomes a fragment in your personal creative loom.

FIND THE PATTERN
Driftloom detects signals across your fragments. That app idea you keep circling? The aesthetic you screenshot every week? The problem you describe three different ways? Driftloom sees the connection.

INCUBATE IDEAS
Choose a creative lens (designer, writer, founder, filmmaker, or game maker) and a direction (app, story, or brand). Driftloom helps you synthesize your fragments into a concept with a clear first move.

BUILD MODE
When you're ready, Build Mode shapes a first-version spec: one screen, one promise, three steps, and a guardrail to keep you focused.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
Star favorites, add follow-up notes, set revisit reminders, search and filter — your fragments stay alive and evolving, not buried in folders.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your ideas are yours.

FREE TO START
• Capture fragments and detect basic patterns on the free tier
• Driftloom Plus unlocks unlimited capture, creative lenses, build plans, and exports — with a 7-day free trial on the annual plan

Your next project is already in your fragments. Driftloom helps you find it.
