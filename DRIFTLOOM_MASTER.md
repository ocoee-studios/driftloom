## What is DriftLoom?

DriftLoom is a premium private dream, idea, and symbol journal. It combines dream capture, lucid dreaming practice, sleep/lunar cycles, recurring symbol insight, creative pattern recognition, and AI-powered dream analysis.

**One-line**: "Capture the fragments. Find the pattern. Turn inner drift into direction."
